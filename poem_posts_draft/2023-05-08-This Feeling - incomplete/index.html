This Feeling - incomplete

Love poems are corny, and the infatuated immature,
I think we can all agree that dealing with feelings is a massive chore
Love’s childish and inefficient, either drama or a bore
And yet I can’t wipe off my stupid grin, cause there’s someone I adore

I’ve gone off the deep end, must’ve hit my head somewhere,
I’ve given up on sleeping, she invades my dreams and nightmares,
Every second vacant, I’m wishing that she were here
I’m new to this feeling,
